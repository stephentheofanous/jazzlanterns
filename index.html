Smooth classics & restyled Pop

The Jazz Lanterns started over a shared love of smooth jazz classics; the likes of Billie Holiday, Gershwin, Ella Fitzgerald and Nina Simone.

It wasn't long though before we started crafting our own soulful jazz reinventions of recent pop, rock and dance hits, so there is something for everyone to enjoy!

The perfect sound for your event

We’re available as a jazz duo, trio or quartet for weddings, private parties, hotel lounge, bar and restaurant sets, and more. Our set list is flexible and we can help you create the perfect sound and atmosphere for your event.


Drop us a message below and we will do our best to get back to you as soon as possible!
